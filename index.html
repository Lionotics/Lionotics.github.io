Welcome!

We are Lionotics, a pair of robotics teams based out of Marsha Stern Talmudical Academy. Our robotics team was first formed in 2011, and has grown to being two teams and over twenty students. Our teams compete in the First Tech Challenge, in which teams design, program, and build a robot that performs an annually assigned task.

Mission Statement

Our team was founded to give students in our school more hands-on opportunities with STEM, and we continue to try to spread this mission. You can learn more about our outreach here

Our Teams

Lionotics was founded in 2011 with FTC team number 5361. After a few years student interest peaked beyond the capacity of the team, so another team was founded with FTC team number 13475. Both remain active, with strong student bodies for each.
